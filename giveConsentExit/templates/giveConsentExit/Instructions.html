Instructions

You are part of a group of {{ Constants.num_players_per_group }} space miners who have been selected to extract minerals from planets around the galaxy. We have identified several planets suitable for mining, each containing varying amounts of elements. Once you mine a planet, you will get a payment that depends on the resources found on that planet. The only way to find your payment is by mining, and you will have {{Constants.num_rounds}} rounds to mine.

You will be assigned to a planet that will deliver a payout between 0 cents and {{ Constants.payout_max }} cents per round. When each round begins, you will see the payment you received by mining that planet. Then, you will have to choose between three options: stay at the same planet, move to a new planet, or return to Earth.

If you choose to stay at the same planet, on the next round you will mine the same planet as the previous round. The payment from the same planet will fluctuate slightly across rounds, due to unexpected circumstances, such as solar storms.

If you choose to move to a new planet , on the next round you will be assigned by ground control to a new planet that is currently not being mined by the rest of the space miners and that you have not mined previously.

If you choose to return to Earth (or allow the game to timeout), you will receive a conciliatory payment for your services, and will not participate in subsequent rounds. This outside-option payment depends on how many rounds you have mined so far, The exact amount will be displayed next to the button.

Each round, you will have {{ Constants.timeout_seconds }} seconds to make your decision. If you allow the game to timeout, by default, you will be sent to Earth.
